The Big CO2 Project

The Big CO2 Project is a project to aware the public about climate change, CO2 levels and room ventilation. By navigating through this website, we hope that you are able to:

  • - gain knowledge about climate change, CO2 monitors and room ventilation
  • - have resources to be able to donate and advocate for these important issues
  • - be able to check the CO2 levels in your classroom
    About Us
    Our mission is to create a resource that informs the public about climate change, CO2 levels and room ventilation.
    Privacy and Ethics
    The Big CO2 Project does not collect user data, rather it displays data from a database that is accessible by the schools that are participating in this project, which they have consented to.
